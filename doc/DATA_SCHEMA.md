## 3. Sampling Logic & GloBE Compliance Truncation

To construct an unbiased micro-panel tracking environment, raw corporate financial statements are filtered through strict truncation rules mimicking the OECD GloBE (Global Anti-Base Erosion) model statutory frameworks:

1. **Revenue Threshold Isolation**: Treated entities are selected based on the 750 Million Euro consolidated revenue threshold defined under Article 1.1 of the GloBE model rules.
2. **Missing Pattern Exclusion**: Observations exhibiting negative or missing `TotalRevenue`, or zero-variance `ETR` data rows are dropped to preserve matrix rank conditions during SLSQP minimization optimization loops.
3. **Outlier Winsorization**: Micro-covariates including `Leverage` and `RD_Intensity` are winsorized at the 1st and 99th percentiles to block anomalous reporting distortions from contaminating the cross-sectional random forest splits.
